Okonomiyaki

Japanese Savory Pancakes

Okonomiyaki is a savory Japanese pancake made with cabbage, batter, and your choice of protein or vegetables. Topped with okonomiyaki sauce, creamy mayonnaise, and bonito flakes, it’s a flavorful and satisfying dish. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or a fun cooking session at home.




At Yoko's Kitchen, we show you step-by-step how to prepare the batter, fold in the ingredients evenly, and cook it to achieve a crispy outside with a soft, fluffy inside.

We also cover creative variations such as adding seafood, mushrooms, or seasonal vegetables. Learn how to plate and garnish Okonomiyaki like a professional chef, making it a visual delight as well as a taste sensation.

Join our hands-on workshops and explore the joy of cooking Okonomiyaki, mastering flipping techniques and flavor combinations that will impress your family and friends.
