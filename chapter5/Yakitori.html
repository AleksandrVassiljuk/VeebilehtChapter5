Yakitori

Classic Japanese Grilled Chicken

Yakitori is a traditional Japanese dish featuring skewered chicken grilled to perfection. Each skewer is brushed with a savory-sweet tare sauce, giving it a rich, caramelized flavor. Perfect as a snack, appetizer, or part of a main course, yakitori brings authentic Japanese taste straight to your table.




At Yoko's Kitchen, we show you how to select the best cuts of chicken, marinate them for maximum flavor, and grill them to achieve that perfect smoky aroma. Learn the techniques to make your yakitori tender and juicy every time.

Our workshops also explore variations such as chicken with spring onions, mushroom skewers, and seasonal vegetable yakitori. We teach tips on presentation, so your skewers not only taste amazing but look visually stunning for parties or family meals.

Join us to discover the joy of grilling like a Japanese chef and impress your friends and family with restaurant-quality yakitori at home.
