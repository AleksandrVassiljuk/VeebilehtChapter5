Tsukune

Japanese Minced Chicken Skewers

Tsukune are tender minced chicken patties, traditionally shaped onto skewers and grilled. Brushed with a sweet and savory tare sauce, they are packed with flavor and perfect as an appetizer or main dish. Easy to prepare at home, tsukune offers a delicious taste of authentic Japanese cuisine.





At Yoko's Kitchen, we teach you how to select the best cuts of chicken, combine them with traditional seasonings, and grill them evenly to achieve that perfect balance of tenderness and smoky flavor.


Variations include adding chopped spring onions, shiitake mushrooms, or a hint of ginger to elevate the taste. We also cover serving suggestions, so your tsukune looks as appealing as it tastes, whether for family dinners or parties.


Join our workshops to master the art of tsukune and bring authentic Japanese flavors straight into your kitchen. Impress your friends and family with these perfectly grilled skewers.
